

  AND, OR, NOT, XOR


  Operation   Boolean Operator   Bitwise Operator
  AND           &&  (and)            &
  OR            ||  (or)             |
  NOT            !  (not)            ~
  XOR              none!             ^

  OR: "At least one or the other"

  AND     True and True --> True
          True and False --> False
          False and False --> False

  NOT     !(True) --> False
            ~1    --> 0
          !(False)--> True
          ~0      --> 1
